Giovanni L. Smith is pursuing a Ph.D. in computer science at North Carolina State University (NCSU) as of fall 2025. His research interests lie in cyber security, embedded-systems and cyber-physical systems. Prior to joining NCSU, he graduated with a B.S. in computer science at Stephen F. Austin State University in 2024.

Smith has used his background in computer science to create tools to help foreign language learners enhance their learning experience. <a href="https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/monolingual-wordreference/oabjacbfhlfigjimkcibfmnkklakdcld?hl=zh-CN&utm_source=ext_sidebar">Monolingual WordReference</a> is a Google Chome extension that hides the native language definitions on a popular bilingual dictionary and currently has over 70 weekly users. <a href="https://github.com/GiovanniSmith/SentenceMiningFormAndScriptsV2">Sentence Mining Form and Scripts</a> is a Windows Form that acts as a control panel to edit the hotkeys and variables of various AutoHotkey scripts, which creates electronic flashcards out of foreign language media.

Smith has spent thousands of hours over nearly a decade learning and polishing his Spanish and Mandarin, and has a <a href="https://www.youtube.com/@giovannismith">YouTube channel</a> with over 3000 subscribers documenting his experience and advice with regards to language learning.
